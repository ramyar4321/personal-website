Video Games

One of my favourite activities is playing video games. Here are a few of my favourites.

Fallout New Vegas is a really great story driven game with rich dialouge, originally created
by Obsidian Studios. It is a role-playing game set in a post apocalyptic world.
If you do get it, make sure to also get the Honest Hearts dlc.

Dark Souls 1 and 3 are the best pieces of medium I have ever
experienced. If you love enviromental storytelling then I am sure
that you will love DS 1 and 3. I haven't played DS 2 so I cannot comment on it.

Last but not least, KOTR 2. This game was also developed by Obsidian.
Not really a Star Wars fan, but I thought I'd give it a try after play NV.
It is very buggy and the mission designs are pretty boring but the characters
and story are very well written.
